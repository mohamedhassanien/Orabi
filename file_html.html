  • Undergraduate Programmes

After high school, live the French business school experience

Choose a programme of excellence, make a commitment to the environment, embrace international opportunities, enjoy a vibrant and exciting campus life, and join a powerful alumni network that will propel your career forward.


  • 3 programmes of excellence: Global BBA, TEMA (for French-speakers), BMS (for French-speakers)
  • A balanced approach between theory and practice
  • 100% guaranteed study placements in the world's top institutions
  • A triple accredited and Top 25-ranked school worldwide

Global BBA

Expand your horizons with the Global BBA. Two tracks: General Track & Global Double Degree CESEM Track.

  • Full time
  • 4 years
  • Paris (Years 1 & 2), Reims, Rouen

TEMA

TEMA is a unique 5-year programme offering an innovative curriculum combining management, technology and creativity, paired with ground-breaking teaching methods.

  • Full time
  • 5 years
  • Paris from the 1st to the 2nd year, Reims

Bachelor in Services Management

Become a manager in retail or gastronomy with the “Bachelor en Management des Services”

  • Apprenticeship
  • 3 years
  • Rouen
